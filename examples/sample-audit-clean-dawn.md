### High Priority (3)

**H3: No preload hint for LCP image**
File: `layout/theme.liquid:78`
Problem: The homepage hero image is the LCP element but there is no `<link rel="preload" as="image">`. Browser only discovers it after parsing the section.
Impact: LCP currently around 2.4s on mid-range mobile. Preload would land it under 2.0s.
Fix: See `before-after.md` § H3.

**S-H4: Twitter Card meta tags missing**
File: `layout/theme.liquid:42-64`
Problem: Open Graph tags present, but no `twitter:card` family. Social previews on X show as small unstyled links. AI scrapers that read Twitter Cards (some Perplexity flows) get a worse signal.
Fix: See `before-after.md` § S-H3 (the RIGHT block includes both OG and Twitter together).

**AEO-H1: Product specs in prose, not `PropertyValue`**
File: `sections/main-product.liquid:142-178`
Problem: Specifications are rendered as a `<table>` with no schema. AI engines parsing the page for "what is the weight of X" rely on PropertyValue.
Fix: See `before-after.md` § AEO-H1.

### What This Theme Does Well (12)

- Skip-to-content link present and styled correctly
- All `<script src>` tags load with `defer`
- Online Store 2.0 architecture — all templates are JSON, sections are composable
- Zero deprecated APIs — `image_url` used consistently, no `{% include %}` anywhere
- Product structured data emitted via `{{ product | structured_data }}` on every PDP
- Canonical handles `?variant=`, `?page=`, and `?ls=` correctly
- Cart drawer implements focus trap with proper `aria-modal` and `inert` toggles
- Image dimensions present on every `<img>` — no CLS from images
- Lazy loading correctly applied below the fold, eager for LCP candidate
- Hreflang correctly emitted across 4 Shopify Markets locales (en-CA, en-US, fr-CA, en-GB)
- FAQ section uses FAQPage schema correctly
- robots.txt explicitly allows GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended
